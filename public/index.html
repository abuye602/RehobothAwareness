Advocacy

​The Rehoboth Awareness Network is dedicated to serving children with disabilities, primarily autism. We work to create a welcoming, faith-based community that embraces individuals with autism and their families.

  • Autism is a spectrum disorder and neurodevelopmental diseases that causes problems in thinking feelings language and social skills

  • Elsabet Daniel

    (Founder and Director of Rehoboth Awareness Network)

    I'm Elsabet Daniel and originally from Ethiopia, East Africa, as well as the founder of Rehoboth Awareness Network. I am a mother of a child with autism, a 2024 graduate of Northern Arizona University in Human Interdisciplinary Behaviour, and a community advocate with over 14 years of experience. Guided by Proverbs - I am committed to empowering faith-based communities and creating inclusive programs that strengthen families.

    Proverbs 31:8 “Speak up for those who cannot speak for themselves.”
